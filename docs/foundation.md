# Foundation

## Mission

Create a durable public process for identifying urgent problems, developing practical solutions, and tracking real execution.

## Non-Goals

Version 1 is not trying to be:

- a full social network
- a native crowdfunding app
- a native labor marketplace
- a perfect identity oracle
- the final form of platform governance

## Version 1 Core

- World locale only
- Issue Board
- Solution Board
- monthly cycle
- hidden live vote totals
- archive instead of delete
- merge distinction notes
- structured completion criteria for winning solutions

## Product Philosophy

- practical over performative
- transparent over hidden
- auditable over ambiguous
- simple before expansive
